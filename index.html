Orthodox Christian Fasting

Simple recipes for every kind of fasting period

This website focuses on recipes for every fasting period that follow both the letter and the spirit of the law. Rules vary across jurisdictions and parishes, so I have tried to include extensive tagging and a robust search system so you can easily filter recipe types.

If you are not an Orthodox Christian, feel free to look at the Learning section to learn more about Orthodoxy and what fasting consists of within the church.
